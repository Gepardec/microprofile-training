Training Microprofile done right

Welcome to the Microprofile done right training, where you will learn the most important Microprofile specifications and how to use them properly. After the training you will know how to use the Microprofile specifications properly, what pitfalls the Microprofile speicifictions have, and how to integrate Micrprofile in your real time applications. The following Microprofile specifications are covered by this training.

  • Microprofile-Config
  • Microprofile-Health
  • Microprofile-Rest-Client
  • Microprofile-OpenAPI
  • Microprofile-Metrics

The training is split into a part where you are introduced to the several Microprofile specifications, and a part where you will lear how the Microprofile specifications interact with each other and how to tackle complex use cases with the learned mechanisms of the Microprofiles specifications.
