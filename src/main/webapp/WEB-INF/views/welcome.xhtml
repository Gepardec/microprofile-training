Welcome

Welcome to the "MicroProfile done right" training, where you will learn the most important MicroProfile specifications and how to use them properly. After the training you will know how to use the MicroProfile specifications properly, what pitfalls the MicroProfile specifications have, and how to integrate MicroProfile in your real time applications. The following MicroProfile specifications are covered by this training.

  • MicroProfile Health 3.0
  • MicroProfile Config 2.0
  • MicroProfile OpenAPI 2.0
  • MicroProfile Rest-Client 2.0
  • MicroProfile Metrics 3.0
  • MicroProfile Open-Tracing 2.0
  • MicroProfile FaultTolerance 3.0
  • MicroProfile JWT 1.2

The training is split into a part where you are introduced to the several MicroProfile specifications, and a part where you will lear how the MicroProfile specifications interact with each other and how to tackle complex use cases with the learned mechanisms of the MicroProfile's specifications.
