Open-Tracing @Traced

The @Traced annotation binds to a CDI Interceptor which can be used to trace method invocations on CDI Beans.

Your task:

The annotation @Traced is meant to be used in com.gepardec.training.microprofile.basic.opentracing.TracedService.
The traced methods shall be called in com.gepardec.training.microprofile.basic.opentracing.TracerController#invoke.
Your traces are visible in the logs and of course in the JaegerUI .

  1. Use the annotation @Traced on class and method level, and see what happens in the JaegerUI.
  2. Use the annotation @Traced on class level and skip the tracing for one of the methods, and take a look into the JaegerUI if it works.
  3. Give one of the traced methods a new operation name, and take a look into the JaegerUI if it works.
