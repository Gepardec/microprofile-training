Open-Tracing @Traced

The @Traced annotation binds to an CDI Interceptor which can be used to trace method invocations on CDI Beans. The implementation is provided by jaeger.io which is also the project behind the JaegerUI.

Your task:

Annotate the class/methods com.gepardec.training.microprofile.basic.opentracing.TracedService and call the method autoTraced from the method invoke several times to produce spans.

Go to the JaegerUI and take a look at your produced spans.
